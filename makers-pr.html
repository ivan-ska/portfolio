Makers PR Website

Makers PR is a Dubai-based agency working with disruptive tech companies. Together, we created a bold and fresh website that reflects the agency's spirit and meets its marketing needs. The client wanted a great-looking site with the ability to edit blog posts and add new pages easily without relying on a developer.

I was responsible for the responsive website design, full development and launch. Check out makers-pr.com to explore the project and get a feel for the agency.

To make the site both beautiful and practical, we chose a flexible web platform. This allowed us to implement custom code solutions where needed, without compromising on usability or visual quality. It also gave the client full control over content updates without touching any code. The site came out fast, reliable and scales well as the agency grows.

We also built a simple blog editor and page templates within the platform, so agency team can quickly publish content and expand the site — all without technical knowledge or extra costs. Blog posts can be added in minutes and new pages follow a clean structure that keeps everything consistent.
