Currency Converter

This project started as a personal solution to a daily annoyance. I was tired of googling exchange rates, managing messy browser tabs, and dealing with ad-heavy converter apps. I wanted something clean, instant, and capable of tracking multiple currencies at once without the clutter.

My curiosity led me to try solving this problem myself. Using my knowledge of UX/UI and vibe coding, I implemented an open-source custom currency converter that can be easily adapted to the needs of anyone.

To start, I worked with the client to define the core attributes and visual direction of the Premium tier. We used brand descriptors and personality sliders to create a "north star" for the project. This framework kept us on track with the visual style, information hierarchy, and overall vibe throughout the process.

I developed a custom visual language where every icon and graphic matches the specific context of the copy. Instead of just filling space, the visuals act as a functional part of the narrative. On the technical side, I maintained strict logic for spacing and interactions. The code is built on a clean, reusable structure rather than a collection of random styles.

The final production-ready page communicates premium value through its execution, not just its copy. The layout adapts logically to any screen size, feeling just as solid on mobile as it does on desktop. I handled the entire project solo—from initial structure discussions and text edits to the final public launch.
